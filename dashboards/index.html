Visualization tools for space-time series analysis with context awareness

Forecasting passenger demand is of great interest for public transport operators. Despite the important role that forecasting play in mobility demand understanding, in-depth transport oriented analysis of the forecasting results is often overlooked, since it raised some challenges.

In this context we developed two visualization tools with open source frameworks that allow to analyze space-time series forecasting with context awareness.

The time visualization tool allows to analyze the forecasting results over large period in all the stops of a transport network and to zoom in for more precise temporal details.

Time visualization

The space visualization tool allows to better understand the passenger demand relations between the different stops of a transport network, and enable a time analysis of the results.
